What is a corporate massage event? This is often performed in a conference room or unused office. Employees are Revitalized A ten to thirty minute chair massage is one way for companies of any size to increase morale and help employees manage their health. In conclusion, employees appreciate the opportunity to easily reduce their stress and pain while at the office.

Chair Massage - also known as seated massage - is a type of massage that takes place on an traditional massage chair, designed to take the weight off the spine and give easy access to the head, neck, back, shoulders and arms.
